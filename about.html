About

Biolve is a B2B supplier of botanical ingredients. This page summarizes sourcing, export documentation, and documentation handling for US customers.

Customer profile

  • US dietary supplement manufacturers: contract and in-house production teams.
  • Distributors: bulk buyers with receiving and documentation requirements.
  • Brands: via established manufacturing and QA channels.

We provide ingredient supply information and documentation upon request.

Sourcing approach

Lot-based supply
Materials are supplied by lot with identifiers used across COA and shipping documents.
Program requirements
Specifications and testing scope are defined by customer requirements and lot program.
Document availability
We disclose what documentation is available for the lot and provide it upon request.

Quality systems & documentation handling

Controlled document handling for supplier qualification and repeat orders.

Document control

NDA workflows are available. Controlled documents are shared through agreed channels.

Batch-level COA

COAs are provided at the batch/lot level. Formats and panels are program-specific.

Traceability

Traceability and chain-of-custody details are shared where available for the lot.

Export & logistics coordination

We coordinate commercial and customs documentation required for import and receiving. Specific requirements depend on destination and customer SOPs.

Common documents

  • Commercial invoice
  • Packing list
  • Certificate of origin (where required)
  • Customs descriptions and harmonized codes as applicable

Receiving alignment

  • Packaging: Coordinated to match receiving requirements where feasible.
  • Lot linkage: Shipment documents aligned to lot identifiers where applicable.
  • Communication: Timelines and paperwork confirmed prior to shipment.

Ongoing B2B supply programs

Repeat orders use the same documentation workflow.
